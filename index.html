Our Services

01 Licensed State Land Surveying

Our team of licensed surveyors specializes in precise staking and laying out of land boundaries in compliance with state regulations. From property boundary surveys to topographic mapping, we ensure the highest standards of accuracy and professionalism.

02 Dry Utilities Staking

Our expertise extends to precise staking and layout of dry utilities, including electric, gas, telecommunications, and more. We ensure accurate positioning of utility infrastructure according to design plans, facilitating seamless integration into development projects.

03 Digital Mapping Services

Utilizing state-of-the-art technology, we offer comprehensive digital mapping services for various applications. From GIS mapping to CAD drafting, our digital mapping solutions provide accurate and detailed representations of geographic data for stakeout purposes.

 

04 Construction Layout Services

Our construction layout services involve precise staking and laying out of structures and infrastructure according to design plans. We facilitate smooth construction processes by ensuring accurate positioning of elements, minimizing errors and optimizing project efficiency.

05 GPS Control Surveys

Our GPS control surveys provide high-precision positioning data for various applications, including construction, land development, and infrastructure projects. We utilize advanced GPS technology to stake out precise locations according to project specifications.

06 Wet Utilities Staking

Our wet utilities staking services involve precise layout of water and wastewater systems according to design plans. From water distribution networks to sewerage systems, we ensure accurate positioning of utility infrastructure for seamless integration into projects.
